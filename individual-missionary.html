About

Jeffery was born in Huntsville, Texas and Juana is from Houston, Texas. They both met while they both were serving in Chi Alpha as small group leaders. Juana was agnostic who found the love of God and His grace over her life -- she gave her life to God and gave Him her heart. She spent 7 years of both her college career and post college in leading more girls like her to Christ.

She completed the Chi Alpha internship and served on staff. After Juana went to Santiago, Chile in 2016/2017, she got her calling from God to serve in overseas missions. Jeffery came to know Christ his freshman year of college at Sam Houston State University. Two years after his conversion, he became a small group leader with Chi Alpha, and had the honor and privilege of leading several young men to Christ. After Graduating college, he joined Chi Alpha as a staff member, and worked as such for five years. It was during a Christian Conference that he felt God call him to missions.
