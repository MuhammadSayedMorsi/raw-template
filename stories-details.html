Assemblies of God World Mission

Engaging the Unreached

Northern Asia

We are called and empowered by God to fulfill the Great Commission in Northern Asia. Our approach is four-fold.

As we lay out this plan you’ll see the term “unreached people group”mentioned several times. This is because unreached people groups, or UPGs, are people groups who have no indigenous community of believing Christians with adequate numbers and resources to evangelize their own people apart from outside assistance.

Unreached people groups are our WHY.

At a glance, this is our HOW — our plan to engage UPGs in Northern Asia:

We live among unreached people groups in order to communicate the Gospel effectively and plant communities of faith while building deep relationships.

We are committed to train, resource and partner with the national body of Christ who currently reside in Northern Asia. God is moving. Through partnership with local believers we can reach people and places that would have been closed to us otherwise.

We engage in college campus life, sharing the Good News of Jesus while developing trusting friendships with students from many unreached people groups.

We respond to the hurting and disenfranchised. Our goal is to give people hope by lifting them to spiritual and physical wholeness.

Are you ready to start a meaningful conversation about how you can be a part of one of our teams in Northern Asia? Let’s talk.

We are so thankful you’re part of this community. Join us on Facebook and Instagram and stay in the know!
